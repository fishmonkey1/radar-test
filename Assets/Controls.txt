Turret control and driving is done with WASD
Cycling through cameras is C
Changing the zoom of the radar is Z
F1 toggles the radar display on and off
Left click fires turret weapons and eventually the machine gun for the driver
R reloads your currently selected ammo
Q and E cycle through options on the screen, or you can use the mouse wheel
T will cycle you through the implemented roles, which is currently a debug feature
Tab will toggle the chat window on and off
